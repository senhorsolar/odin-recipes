Banana Bread

Description

This is a homemade recipe for banana bread. You can optionally add milk if so desired.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoons of baking soda
  • 3/4 cups of rolled oats
  • 2/3 cups of sugar
  • 2 teaspoons of cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup of oil (canola)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 3 medium bananas
  • 1 cup of milk (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. Mix all dry ingredients
  3. Mix all wet ingredients
  4. Mix wet and dry ingredients
  5. Bake for one hour
