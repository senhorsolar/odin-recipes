Rice

Description

This is a recipe for Brazilian style white rice. Basically it contains a lot of garlic.

Ingredients

  • White rice (long grain)
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mince garlic
  2. Saute garlic in olive oil
  3. Place white rice in same pan
  4. Let rice cook for 1 min while stirring
  5. Add water until it fully covers rice
  6. Let rice cook until desired softness
