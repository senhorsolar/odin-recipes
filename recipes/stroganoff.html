Stroganoff

Description

This is a recipe for chicken stroganoff, Brazilian style. It is common in Brazil to use rice instead of pasta. Other than that I'm not sure what the difference is.

The reciple should feed four people, or two if you like hearty portions like myself.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 whole onions
  • 1/2 whole garlic
  • 1 can creme de leite
  • paprika
  • garlic and onion powder (optional)
  • salt
  • Rice
  • mini potato sticks

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into dices
