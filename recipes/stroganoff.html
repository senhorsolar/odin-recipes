Stroganoff

Description

This is a recipe for chicken stroganoff, Brazilian style. It is common in Brazil to use rice instead of pasta. Other than that I'm not sure what the difference is.

The reciple should feed four people, or two if you like hearty portions like myself.

Ingredients

  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 2 whole onions
  • 1/2 whole garlic
  • 1 can creme de leite
  • paprika (optional)
  • BBQ sauce (optional)
  • garlic and onion powder (optional)
  • salt
  • Rice
  • mini potato sticks
  • One large pan for cooking

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breast into dices
  2. Mince garlic and cut onions
  3. Add oil to large pan and place on high temperature
  4. Saute onions until golden
  5. Add garlic and chicken
  6. Allow chicken to fry until it looks semi-cooked, stir frequently throughout
  7. After, add water and desired seasoning and let chicken fry again
  8. Once the water begins to evaporate, add can of creme de leite (required) and BBQ (optional)
  9. Put temperature on low and stir
  10. Add to plate with rice and potato sticks
